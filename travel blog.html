SKANDAGIRI HILLS

POPULARITY

Skandagiri Hills Trek, also known as Kalavara Durga, is a popular destination for adventure seekers and nature lovers near Bangalore.

DISTANCE FROM CITY CENTER

Distance from City Center: 70 kilometers (approx. 2 hours drive).

BEST TIME TO VISIT

The best time to visit Skandagiri Hills is during the winter months (October-February), when the weather is cooler and ideal for trekking..
